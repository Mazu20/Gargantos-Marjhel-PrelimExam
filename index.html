About Me

Hi, I’m Marjhel M. Gargantos, also known as Mazu in the art community. I'm an artist and a storyteller passionate about bringing ideas to life through illustration and comics. I create video tutorials on YouTube and Tiktok to help fellow aspiring creators learn how to upload and share their series on Webtoon, Tapas, and Namicomi. Outside of that, I regularly share my artworks across Instagram, TikTok, Tumblr, Facebook, and YouTube, where I enjoy connecting with fellow artists, readers and fans.

I’m a former architecture student now pursuing a degree in animation, and I'm now blending my technical background with my love for creativity. As a Webtoon creator, I focus on crafting stories that inspire and entertain, while continuing to grow my artistic journey. I also take commissions from time to time, though they’re currently closed as I focus on my studies and college life.

  • Medium: Digital illustrations
  • Focus: Concept Arts, Fan Arts, Comic
  • Currently not available for art commissions and collaborations

Series Title: The Prince's Path To A Leisure Life (2023)

Flynn Julian Von Roseville, The first prince of the Atheria Empire has a past life where he was just a average teen girl. As she got reincarnated as a prince, he immediately sought out his father, thinking he would experience parental love, but the only thing he got was betrayal and death. Was death the end? No, he regressed back in time, but he still ended up being killed. He had given up the goal to be acknowledged by his father and now wishes for a peaceful life.
